**Goodweek Tires – Multi-Segment Capital Budgeting Analysis**
**Overview**

This project evaluates the financial feasibility of entering both the Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) and Replacement Market (RM) segments using a structured capital budgeting framework.

**Market Assumptions
OEM Market:**

2 million vehicles in Year 1

2.5% annual growth

4 tires per vehicle

11% projected market share

$36 selling price per tire

$18 variable cost per tire

**Replacement Market:**

16 million tire market size

2% annual growth

8% projected market share

$59 selling price per tire

$18 variable cost per tire

**Financial Model Components:**

Revenue forecasting by segment

Contribution margin analysis

Working capital estimation

Net Present Value (NPV)

Internal Rate of Return (IRR)

Sensitivity analysis

Scenario analysis


**Repository Contents:**

goodweek-tires-financial-model.xlsx

executive-summary.pdf

**Objective**

To determine whether expansion into both markets creates shareholder value under multiple operating scenarios.
